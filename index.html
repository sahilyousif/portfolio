SAHIL ANAND

Researcher, Designer and Technologist

I design, build and study interactions with robots and physical computing systems. Currently, I'm pursuing my Master's degree in Human Centered Design and Engineering at the University of Washington, Seattle.

ABOUT ME

My research interests include Human-Robot Interactions, Physical Computing, Social and Embodied Interactions, Augmented Reality and Natural User Interfaces. Before making my transition into UX, I worked as a Full-Stack Engineer and Designer for 6 years. I worked for a technology startup where I explored new and emerging technologies and created high-fidelity prototypes.

I enjoy collaborating across disciplines and designing interactions for emerging technologies. Over the course of my career, I have acquired a very broad set of skills, and I enjoy applying them to a wide variety of design challenges, and picking things up along the way.
