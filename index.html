ABOUT ME

..with Rosie at the Human Centered Robotics Lab in UW

I'm currently a graduate student in Human Centered Design and Engineering at the University of Washington, Seattle. I design, build and research interactions with robots and physical computing systems.

My research interests include Human-Robot Interactions, Social and Embodied Interactions, Physical Computing, Augmented Reality and Natural User Interfaces. I enjoy collaborating across disciplines and designing interactions for emerging technologies. Over my course of my career, I have acquired a very broad set of skills, and I enjoy applying them to a wide variety of design challenges, and picking things up along the way.
